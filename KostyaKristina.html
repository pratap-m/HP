About Us

Kostya & Kristina Mershenko

Worship Leaders

Our family moved from Ukraine to Czech Republic in 2019. Everything around has changed. Except our heart for God and worship. Our experience of being worship team leaders is around 16 years.


We created music bands, composed own songs and recorded several albums. Besides that, we started a teenage and youth ministry and led it around 10 years.


Investing into people, helping them grow and be closer to God is our passion. And we know that worship is a great tool to do it at any age age in any country.

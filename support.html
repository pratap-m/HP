Supporting Others

Our church is founded on the principle of love and support for others,

including providing financial assistance to those who need it most. That's

why we've created this page called "Financially supporting others," where

you can find a list of projects we officially support from all over the world

and where you can contribute to help those in need.


We believe that together we can provide hope and change the lives of

those who are in difficult situations. Thank you for your support.

Get Involved

Connect with others like you

Life can get complicated and tough sometimes and then there is the

happy times worth sharing with others. During all these moments, it is

best to walk with people who you can speak & connect with, who can

encourage you or pray for you and also celebrate with you in the good

times.


We encourage you to check out some belong groups we have and sign up

to join and connect with other believers just like you.

how to get involved

Belong Groups

Our small groups meet in different parts of the city within the week to fellowship, form life long bonds & encourage each other to grow in our faith. We invite you to join one or more of the belong groups which you think would be the best for you. The groups we currently have running are

  • English speaking Belong groups through out the city
  • Czech speaking Belong group
  • Young Adults (for 18 to 35 years old)
  • Youth Group
  • Women’s Group
  • Men’s Group

Kids Ministry

Our kids are the future generation of the church and it is very important that they get to learn about the biblical principals & to be grounded in Christ from a very young age.

We have some really fun activities which are taking place for kids of all ages & during these events, the kids can play and form bonds with other kids & it is an opportunity for also connecting with other parents going through the same parenting things as you.

The kid’s programs are all free to attend in our Heart Prague church. Here are the current running programs

  • Messy Church (For all kids - Bible stories, singing, dancing, eating, arts & crafts) - 1 Saturday morning per month
  • Playgroup for 1 to 4 years old & parents on Mondays
  • Mothers Group (For Moms & little babies) on Wednesdays

Become an HP Member

Once you feel comfortable and at home, we would love you to become an official Heart Prague church member.

under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.

Through your donation, we can do so much more in Heart Prague for our

community and also to support missions spreading the gospel.

In-Person Giving

During the Sunday service, there is an opportunity to give either in cash or using a payment card terminal for Sunday offering. An offering basket will passed around during the announcements segment of the service.

Cafe Charity Donations

Before and after service, you can get a coffee, tea or hot chocolate from our cafe. You pay through donation with all proceeds each month going to a chosen charity or mission for the month. Not only will you get a nice hot drink, but you also help support in spreading the gospel and helping others.

It is possible to put cash in the donation jar, or donate through a payment card terminal.

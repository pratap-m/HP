our beliefs

We believe in God, the Father Almighty, Creator of Heaven and Earth;

and in Jesus Christ, His only Son Our Lord, Who was conceived by the

Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, suffered under Pontius Pilate, was

crucified, died, and was buried. He descended into Hell; the third day He

rose again from the dead; He ascended into Heaven, and sitteth at the

right hand of God, the Father almighty; from thence He shall come to

judge the living and the dead. I believe in the Holy Spirit, the Christian

Church, the communion of saints, the forgiveness of sins, the

resurrection of the body and life everlasting.


Amen.


Heart Prague is affiliated with the Assemblies of God and the Apostolic

Church of the Czech Republic.

Our Values

Holiness

Because we love Jesus, we strive to live lives that bring glory and honor to Him. We desire to know Him deeply and become like Him, so we can make Him known. We are to be holy as He is holy.

Corinthians 6:17; Peter 3:11

Hope

We believe that Jesus is the answer to problems that this world faces and is the one that came to reconcile us back to God. Through Jesus, we are forgiven, restored and set apart for a life of purpose. Our responsibility is to always be ready to give the reason for this hope that we have.

1Peter 3:15

Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit empowers the follower of christ to be a witness and to live a holy life. Our desire is to provide opportunities for people to encounter the Holy Spirit. The Holy Spirit draws people to God, opening their hearts to receive Him, grow in their faith and then love others.

John 14:15-17,25-26

Humanity

God so loved the world. Each and every person matters to God so they matter to us. We are a church for all people, for all nations. Regardless of whether one believes or not, they are welcome at Heart Prague. We want to provide a place to belong, a place to be loved and a place for people to explore the truths of God’s Word.

John 3:16-17; Romans 10:13-15

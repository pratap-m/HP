more about our sundays

The heart of our church is to love Jesus first and the most.

With love for Jesus and from Jesus, we will love the people

that share our city and our world in both word and deed.

What does our sunday look like

Worship

The service will start with a greeting followed by singing a few worship songs. The music will be upbeat and contemporary. You are invited to join us and sing.

Sermon

After worship, someone will share a message from the Bible. he teaching will always relate to Jesus. He is at the Heart of all we do.

Announcements and Prayers

At the end, there will be announcements, prayers or special planned activities for the service. Even if you come late, please come in. It is okay. We are glad you came!

Kids Sunday School

We want children to enjoy coming to church. We have a Sunday school service just for kids up to 6 years in the kids room. They can expect a fun program of bible teaching, games and activities focused on children.

how to attend sunday service

Livestream Service

You are also able to join us each Sunday at 10 AM CEST for the Sunday

service being streamed live from our Facebook and Youtube page.

You will get to enjoy worship, preaching and also announcements.


When you join, we would love to say Hi in the comments section.

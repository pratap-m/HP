Growth Track

The Track guides you through the discovery of your God-given purpose and toward the life He has created you to live. The Growth Track is made up of four steps that equip you to Know God, Get Connected, Discover Purpose, and Make a Difference.

The Christianity Explored

This is a perfect opportunity for anyone who is curious about Christianity or seeking to deepen their understanding of the faith. During this course, we will delve into the basics of Christianity, including the life and teachings of Jesus, the Bible, and the significance of the cross.
